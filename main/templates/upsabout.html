About Jelajah Didik Pahang

KPMIM Tourism Cooperative is a student organization established to provide high-quality and affordable travel opportunities to the community of higher education and school students. We adhere to cooperative principles that promote collaboration, integrity, and excellent customer service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer educational and enjoyable travel experiences while fostering awareness of local history, culture, and natural beauty. We are also committed to providing students with opportunities to develop management and leadership skills through involvement in tourism activities.

Our Vision

To become a leader in providing affordable and informative travel packages for students, as well as promoting local tourism and culture.
